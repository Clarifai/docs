# Creating and Training Models

**Learn how to easily create and train new models**
<hr />

The Clarifai platform simplifies the entire process of creating and training AI models, making it incredibly easy and efficient. With just a single click, your model is not only trained but also automatically deployed, ready to enhance your business solutions instantly.

You can either [build custom models](https://docs.clarifai.com/portal-guide/model/clarifai-models) tailored to your specific needs or jumpstart your projects with Clarifai's [pre-optimized models](https://docs.clarifai.com/portal-guide/model/pcustom-model-walkthrough), which are designed for immediate use.
